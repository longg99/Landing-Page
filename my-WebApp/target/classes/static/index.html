Lots of Rental Choices

We are partnering with hundreds of rental property owners across the City of Toronto (and the GTA soon). As a result, RENTTO can offer you a wide range of rental housing to choose from. We will try to find a list of housing that best matches your budget and preferences. Whether it is a house, a condo, an apartment, a basement or a room in a shared house, we got you covered!

Cost of Service: Free!

Renting in Toronto can be extremely costly. We know and understand that, so our service is completely free :) RENTTO will try to help you find your next place to live, without breaking the bank. We have many budget-friendly options to choose from. We also have high-end places if you want to go higher!

Our Service is for Everyone

Toronto is for Everyone. So is RENTTO. Canadians, (international) students, (new) immigrants, work-permit holders, you name it! No matter who you are, where you came from, what your background is, we are here to help you to look for your new home. We can even support people with no or bad credit!

Easy to Use

RENTTO is super easy to use. You can either fill out a form or give us a call and we can do it together. That form contains your basic information and preferences on budget, location, house type and so on. Also, we take privacy seriously so you don't need to worry! Your information will only be shared with the landlord after you choose a place.
